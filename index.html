How It Works

Four simple steps from setup to smooth operator.

1

Quick Setup

Grant accessibility & overlay permissions, pick your AI provider, and enter your API key. Takes under a minute.

2

Open Aisle

RizzBot activates automatically when you open the Aisle dating app. A floating Rizz button appears on your chat screen.

3

Sync Their Profile

Visit your match's profile and tap Sync. RizzBot uses their bio, interests, and Q&A to personalize every reply.

4

Tap & Reply

Tap the Rizz bubble to get 4 reply suggestions — one per vibe. Copy to clipboard or paste directly into the chat.
