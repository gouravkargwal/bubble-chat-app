Installation Guide

Follow these steps to get RizzBot up and running on your Android device.

🛡️

Disable Play Protect

Google Play Protect blocks apps installed outside the Play Store. Temporarily disable it to install RizzBot.

  1. Open the Google Play Store app
  2. Tap your profile icon (top right)
  3. Tap "Play Protect"
  4. Tap the gear icon (top right)
  5. Toggle OFF "Scan apps with Play Protect"
💡 You can re-enable Play Protect after installing the app.
📥

Install the APK

Download and install the RizzBot APK file on your device.

  1. Open the APK file from your downloads
  2. If prompted, tap "Settings" to allow installs from this source
  3. Toggle ON "Allow from this source"
  4. Go back and tap "Install"
  5. Wait for installation to complete
🔓

Allow Restricted Settings

Android 13+ restricts accessibility for sideloaded apps. You need to allow it manually.

  1. Open device Settings
  2. Go to "Apps" and find "RizzBot"
  3. Tap the three-dot menu (top right)
  4. Tap "Allow restricted settings"
  5. Enter your device PIN/pattern if asked
♿

Enable Accessibility Service

RizzBot uses accessibility to read chat messages on Aisle and show reply suggestions.

  1. Open device Settings → "Accessibility"
  2. Find "RizzBot" in the list
  3. Tap it and toggle ON
  4. Confirm the permission dialog
🗂️

Allow Display Over Other Apps

Overlay permission lets RizzBot show the floating bubble and suggestions on top of Aisle.

  1. Open device Settings → "Apps"
  2. Find "RizzBot" and tap it
  3. Tap "Display over other apps"
  4. Toggle ON
🚀

You're All Set!

Open Aisle, go to any chat, and the Rizz button will appear automatically. Tap it to get AI-powered reply suggestions!

Help & FAQ

Answers to common questions about RizzBot.

What is RizzBot? ▼
RizzBot is an AI-powered dating reply assistant for the Aisle app. It reads your chat conversations and generates 4 personalized reply suggestions in different vibes — Flirty, Witty, Smooth, and Bold — delivered via a floating bubble overlay.
Is RizzBot free? ▼
Yes, RizzBot is completely free to use. You just need to bring your own API key from a supported AI provider. Groq offers free API keys with generous rate limits, making it the best option to get started at zero cost.
Which AI providers are supported? ▼
RizzBot supports 4 AI providers: Groq (free & fast, recommended), OpenAI, Claude (Anthropic), and Google Gemini. You can switch between providers anytime from the app settings.
How do I get an API key? ▼
Visit your chosen provider's website: Groq — console.groq.com, OpenAI — platform.openai.com, Claude — console.anthropic.com, Gemini — aistudio.google.com. Sign up, navigate to API keys, and create a new key. Paste it into RizzBot's settings.
Does RizzBot read my other apps? ▼
No. RizzBot's accessibility service exclusively monitors the Aisle dating app. It does not read, access, or interact with any other app on your phone. The overlay automatically hides when you leave Aisle.
The bubble doesn't appear — what do I do? ▼
Make sure both permissions are enabled: (1) Accessibility Service — Settings → Accessibility → RizzBot → ON, and (2) Overlay — Settings → Apps → RizzBot → Display over other apps → ON. Also ensure the service toggle is enabled inside the RizzBot app. If you're on Android 13+, you may need to "Allow restricted settings" first (see Setup Guide above).
Replies aren't generating — what's wrong? ▼
Check that: (1) Your API key is valid — use the "Validate Key" button in settings, (2) You have an internet connection, (3) Your API provider isn't rate-limiting you. If using Groq's free tier, wait a moment and try again. You can also try switching to a different provider.
How do I uninstall RizzBot? ▼
First disable the accessibility service: Settings → Accessibility → RizzBot → OFF. Then uninstall normally: Settings → Apps → RizzBot → Uninstall. All local data is deleted with the app.

Privacy Policy

Effective: March 5, 2026

Overview

RizzBot ("the App") is a locally-installed Android application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the App accesses, how it is used, and your rights.

Data We Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All data remains on your device.

What the App Accesses

  • Accessibility Service: Used exclusively to read chat messages and UI elements within the Aisle dating app. The service does not read, monitor, or interact with any other application on your device.
  • Overlay Permission: Used to display the floating bubble and suggestion cards on top of the Aisle app.

API Keys

Your API keys are stored locally on your device using Android's encrypted SharedPreferences. They are never transmitted to our servers. Keys are sent only to your chosen AI provider (Groq, OpenAI, Anthropic, or Google) to generate reply suggestions.

AI Provider Communication

When you request reply suggestions, the App sends conversation context (chat messages) directly to your selected AI provider's API. This data is subject to your chosen provider's privacy policy. We do not proxy, intercept, or store this communication.

Local Storage

The App stores conversation history, synced profiles, and settings in a local SQLite database and DataStore on your device. You can clear all data at any time from the App's settings. All data is permanently deleted when you uninstall the App.

Analytics & Crash Reporting

The App uses Firebase Crashlytics to collect anonymous crash reports that help us fix bugs. This includes device type, OS version, and crash stack traces. No personal data, conversation content, or API keys are included in crash reports.

Third-Party Services

  • AI Providers: Groq, OpenAI, Anthropic (Claude), Google (Gemini) — subject to their respective privacy policies
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Anonymous crash reporting — subject to Google's privacy policy

Children's Privacy

RizzBot is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
