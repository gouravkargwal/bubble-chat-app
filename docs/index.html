Installation Guide

Follow these steps to get Cookd up and running on your Android device.

🔑

Install and sign in

Install Cookd and continue with Google to authenticate your account.

  1. Open Cookd
  2. Tap Continue with Google
  3. Finish onboarding setup
🗔️

Grant overlay permission

Overlay is required so the bubble can appear above your chat screen.

  1. Tap Grant Permission in app
  2. Open Display over other apps for Cookd
  3. Turn it ON
📸

Capture when needed

Tap the bubble and capture screenshots only when you want AI suggestions.

  1. Tap the floating bubble
  2. Confirm screenshot capture consent
  3. Generate suggestions from the captured image
🚀

You're All Set!

Open your chat app, use the bubble flow, and send the suggestion you like.

Help & FAQ

Answers to common questions about Cookd.

What is Cookd? ▼
Cookd is an AI-powered dating reply assistant. It analyzes chat and profile screenshots to generate suggestions you can quickly copy and send.
Is Cookd free? ▼
Cookd offers a free tier plus paid upgrades for higher limits and premium tools.
Do I need special system permissions? ▼
Cookd's core flow only requires overlay and user-triggered capture consent.
Does Cookd read my other apps? ▼
Cookd uses user-triggered screenshot capture and overlay UI only when you initiate it.
The bubble doesn't appear — what do I do? ▼
Make sure overlay permission is enabled and the service toggle is active in the app. Reopen Cookd after granting permission.
Replies aren't generating — what's wrong? ▼
Check internet connectivity, ensure screenshot capture completed, then retry. If backend traffic is high, wait a few seconds and try again.
How do I uninstall Cookd? ▼
Uninstall from Android settings. You can also delete account data first from Settings → Privacy & Data.
